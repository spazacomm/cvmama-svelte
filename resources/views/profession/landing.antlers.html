Service Overview

How do your reverse recruiting services work?

Our Reverse Recruiting services streamline your job search by enlisting a team of career professionals to handle the legwork on your behalf. Here's a breakdown of our services:

Marketing Materials: Before your job search begins, your Resume Writer will collaborate with you to develop impactful marketing materials specifically tailored to your job search. Your resume, cover letter, and LinkedIn profile will be expertly optimized to enhance your success.

Job Search: Your Job Search Specialist scours for job opportunities based on your career preferences. You have the final say in approving or rejecting any jobs we find. You are also encouraged to find and share jobs directly with your team.

Application Process: Since you have better things to do than sit on your computer filling out job applications, your Job Application Specialist will submit each and every job application on your behalf. We'll ensure each application is completed thoroughly and accurately.

Networking: Your Reverse Recruiter will utilize your LinkedIn profile and a dedicated job search email to identify and connect with hiring managers and decision makers in your field. Our networking efforts are completely customized to your industry and profession, and are designed with the goal of landing you interviews.

Interview Preparation: Once we secure you interviews, our Career Coaches are available to provide interview training, salary negotiation guidance, and insights on the company and interviewers.

Ongoing Support: You will have access to our exclusive job search portal, providing total insight into your daily job search activity. You can also communicate directly with your Reverse Recruiter and/or Career Coach through our job search portal every weekday during our regular business hours.

For more information, please visit Our Process page.

Client Support

What types of problems can you help solve?

We have a proven track record of assisting numerous job seekers in securing employment in a variety of executive-level roles. Here are the types of challenges our reverse recruiting services can address:

Time Management: If you are currently employed, dedicating time to job searching after a full day of work can be overwhelming. Our services save you time by allowing a Reverse Recruiter to handle your job search for you.

Professional Job Search: While you excel in your profession, navigating the job market may not be your expertise. Our Reverse Recruiters specialize in job searching, staying updated on the latest trends and strategies to ensure an effective approach to your job search.

Smooth Career Transitions: Transitioning to a new career path can be challenging, especially if you lack experience in a specific industry or role. Our experts excel at highlighting your transferable skills and developing custom networks to facilitate a seamless transition.

Unknown Job Markets: Securing a job in an unfamiliar location can be like learning to speak another language. Hiring managers have different hiring expectations depending on the location. We know how to position our clients based on their unique geographic requirements.

Finding Specialized Jobs: Seeking highly specialized job opportunities can be daunting as they are not always publicly advertised. Our Reverse Recruiters possess the knowledge and resources to locate niche job openings in the "hidden" executive job market.

Addressing Ageism and Discrimination: In today's job market, ageism and discrimination are unfortunate realities. Our team is well-equipped to guide you in navigating these challenges and avoiding discriminatory hiring practices.

Standing Out in Competitive Fields: Executive-level positions are highly competitive, with numerous talented professionals vying for the same roles. We understand the dynamics of stiff competition and can assist you in distinguishing yourself from other applicants effectively.
