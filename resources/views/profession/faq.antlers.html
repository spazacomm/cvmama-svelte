Reverse Recruiting

Reverse recruiting is a specialized service designed to assist busy professionals in delegating their job search to the experts. Introduced in 2015, this innovative approach involves a dedicated reverse recruiter who actively seeks out suitable job opportunities, applies to positions on behalf of the client, and networks to secure interviews.

Service Overview

How do your reverse recruiting services work?

Our Reverse Recruiting services streamline your job search by enlisting a team of career professionals to handle the legwork on your behalf. Here's a breakdown of our services:

Marketing Materials: Before your job search begins, your Resume Writer will collaborate with you to develop impactful marketing materials specifically tailored to your job search. Your resume, cover letter, and LinkedIn profile will be expertly optimized to enhance your success.

Job Search: Your Job Search Specialist scours for job opportunities based on your career preferences. You have the final say in approving or rejecting any jobs we find. You are also encouraged to find and share jobs directly with your team.

Application Process: Since you have better things to do than sit on your computer filling out job applications, your Job Application Specialist will submit each and every job application on your behalf. We'll ensure each application is completed thoroughly and accurately.

Networking: Your Reverse Recruiter will utilize your LinkedIn profile and a dedicated job search email to identify and connect with hiring managers and decision makers in your field. Our networking efforts are completely customized to your industry and profession, and are designed with the goal of landing you interviews.

Interview Preparation: Once we secure you interviews, our Career Coaches are available to provide interview training, salary negotiation guidance, and insights on the company and interviewers.

Ongoing Support: You will have access to our exclusive job search portal, providing total insight into your daily job search activity. You can also communicate directly with your Reverse Recruiter and/or Career Coach through our job search portal every weekday during our regular business hours.

Service Overview

What is the writing process and turnaround time?

The resume writing process typically takes about 2 weeks to complete and receive your final documents. This includes some expected back-and-forth revisions to get your resume just right.

Here’s what you can expect when signing up for our resume writing services:

  1. Purchase the appropriate resume package and add-on services on our website.
  2. Immediately after purchasing, you will receive an email requesting your current resume (if available) and any supporting documents like previous job descriptions, letters of recommendation, awards/certifications, etc. (If you do not receive this email within 5 minutes of ordering, please check your spam folder).
  3. Once you send over your info, we will review your resume and assign you the most qualified writer within one business day.
  4. Your professional resume writer will contact you to schedule a 1-on-1 phone consultation in order to gather any remaining information needed to craft your resume.
  5. Following the phone consultation, you can expect to receive your custom resume within 3-5 business days (2 business days for priority service).
  6. Once you receive your expertly crafted resume, you can arrange another 1-on-1 phone consultation with your resume writer to discuss any necessary edits or questions you might have.
  7. We commit to working with you by providing unlimited edits/revisions for one week after you receive your draft.
  8. Your final resume will be delivered in both Word and PDF formats.

If you ordered additional services such as a LinkedIn profile, cover letter, or professional bio, you can expect to receive these documents within 2 business days of approving your final resume. It’s essential for us to have final approval on your resume before we begin working on additional documents.

How do I know which resume category to select?

Choosing the right resume category is crucial to ensure that you are matched with a resume writer whose expertise aligns with your career goals. Here are the details for each category:

C-Level Writing: Tailored for current or aspiring C-level executives, including positions like CEO, COO, CTO, CMO, CSO, CRO, and others.

Executive Writing: Customized for executives aiming for director-level positions or higher. If you are pursuing roles at the director or vice president level, this package is well-suited for you.

Professional Writing: Ideal for mid-career professionals with 3+ years of experience in any field. Whether you have 3 years of experience or more, the professional category is the best fit if you are not at the director level or above.

Entry-Level Writing: Tailored for individuals with 0-2 years of experience. If you have more than 2 years of experience, even if it's in a different industry, the entry-level category may not be suitable for you.

Federal Writing: Designed for employees looking to transition into federal government roles or advance in their federal careers.

If you’re still unsure what category to choose, please contact our Customer Success team at (831) 888-0967 or help@findmyprofession.com.

What is ATS and are your resumes ATS compatible?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System, a software tool that streamlines recruitment and hiring processes electronically. This system can be utilized online by organizations of varying sizes, catering to both enterprise and small-business requirements.

Every resume we create is optimized for ATS compatibility, enhancing your chances of reaching the hiring manager. Our resume writers prioritize a clean and readable format, avoiding excessive designs or graphics that could impede ATS scanning. Furthermore, we strategically integrate relevant keywords to boost ATS compatibility, ensuring your resume stands out to potential employers.

Service Overview

How do your career coaching services work?

After selecting and purchasing the sessions that best meet your needs, you will be directed to create an account in our online portal. Shortly after your purchase, you will be assigned to a Career Coach. You can then schedule your call(s) and communicate with your coach directly through our portal.

Interview Preparation: Schedule a 90-minute Zoom call with your Career Coach.

Pre-Interview Report: Schedule a 30-minute Zoom call. You will be asked for details related to your interview so your Career Coach can prepare your report prior to your call.

Salary Negotiation: Schedule your 90-minute Zoom call with your Career Coach.

What should I expect during a coaching session?

Each session is conducted via Zoom with your Career Coach at a pre-scheduled time.

Interview Preparation: These sessions provide essential tips for interview preparation, including etiquette, establishing a cultural fit, answering common questions, and managing nervousness. You can also participate in a mock interview to practice your skills. Sessions can be scheduled for general interview support or tailored for an upcoming interview.

Pre-Interview Report: Your Career Coach will prepare and review a detailed report that includes key information about the company, enhancing your interview responses. You’ll learn about the company’s social media presence, recent news updates, salary information, potential interview questions, and more.

Salary Negotiation: Discover how to assess your worth and effectively communicate it to potential employers. You will discover common pitfalls that job seekers encounter, strategies for responding to negative feedback from employers, and techniques for signing an offer without leaving money on the table. Sessions can be scheduled for general salary negotiation guidance or customized to focus on a specific job offer.

How many coaching sessions will I have?

Each coaching session is offered a la carte, ensuring you're not tied to a specific duration or commitment. Our sessions are designed to achieve their goals within a single session. If you desire further coaching, you’re welcome to purchase additional sessions as needed.
