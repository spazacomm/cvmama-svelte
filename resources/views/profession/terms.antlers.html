Reverse Recruiting 6-Month Job Offer Guarantee

We are pleased to offer a job offer guarantee with our Premier and Elite Reverse Recruiting packages. If you do not receive a job offer or at least 5 interviews after using our services for 6 consecutive months, we will extend your services at no cost until you do receive a job offer or a total of 5 interviews, for up to another 6 months.

A job offer is defined as an offer of employment from an employer to you, regardless of whether you accept it. An interview refers to a formal meeting with any individual involved in the hiring decision for a position or target company that you have approved, including but not limited to recruiters and hiring managers. Each interview counts towards the guarantee, irrespective of your attendance. Find My Profession maintains discretion regarding whether an interview qualifies for the guarantee.

To maintain eligibility for the job offer guarantee, you must:

  • Use our Premier or Elite Reverse Recruiting services (including our resume writing services) for a total of 6 consecutive service months without any gaps.
  • Maintain the same job target goals and utilize the same Find My Profession resume throughout your service.
  • Respond to messages from your Reverse Recruiter (RR) within 48 hours.
  • Attend all scheduled interviews and networking calls.
  • Approve or reject job opportunities presented by the RR within 48 hours.
  • Participate in at least two weekly Zoom meetings per month with your RR.

Delays in these commitments or lack of client engagement that impact the job search process will void the guarantee. Find My Profession reserves the right to deem a client ineligible for this guarantee if the client’s actions or decisions prevent us from fulfilling the service requirements. This includes, but is not limited to, rejecting job opportunities that align with the client’s established search criteria and expectations. Find My Profession has sole discretion in determining whether a client meets the eligibility requirements for this guarantee, based on their search criteria, engagement level, and overall expectations.

The job offer guarantee activates after 6 service months. If a job offer or the expected number of interviews is not secured during this period, you may qualify for an additional 6 months of free support, provided that all conditions are met. For example, if you receive one qualified interview during the initial 6 months, services will continue at no cost until a job offer or the remaining four required interviews are secured, for up to an additional 6 months. Once the guarantee conditions are fulfilled or a job offer is received, services will conclude without additional fees. Eligibility is formally reviewed by leadership only after six consecutive months of service, once all criteria have been verified.

Resume Writing 60-Day Interview Guarantee

We offer a straightforward 60-day interview guarantee. If you do not receive noticeably more interviews after 60 days of using the resume we created for you, while applying for jobs that match your resume’s job target, we will rewrite your resume one time at no additional charge.

If you meet these application guidelines and still do not see a significant increase in interview opportunities, please contact us at help@findmyprofession.com to initiate the revision process.
